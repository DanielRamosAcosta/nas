# Ficha de producto Jonsbo N3 — 8-Bay NAS Mini Aluminum Chassis

Esta es la ficha de producto del fabricante Jonsbo para la caja modelo N3. Cubre las especificaciones oficiales del producto, no necesariamente la configuración exacta instalada en nuestro NAS.

---

## Resumen de características

- 8+1 Bay expansion (8x 3.5" HDD + 1x 2.5" SSD)
- USB3.2 Gen2 Type-C (10Gbps) frontal
- Mini Size (233mm x 262mm x 298mm)
- Front panel detachable grille
- Standard configuration 100mm x2 fans
- Mini tower radiator support

## Product Overview

The N3 shell is made of an integrated aluminum magnesium alloy material, with a delicate sand-blasting process on the surface and a strong metallic texture. It adopts an upper and lower compartment structure design. The motherboard compartment supports ITX motherboards and full height expansion cards, as well as MINI tower heat sinks. The front panel ventilation holes are paired with rear window fans to provide a good heat dissipation solution. The hard disk compartment supports 8-bay hard disk installation, and the front Type-C interface is used for high-speed data transmission.

## Storage

### 3.5" HDD Bays

Front 8x 3.5-inch hard drive bays for high capacity storage.

### 2.5" SSD Bay

2.5-inch bay can adapt to SSD high-speed hard drives. Extremely fast response for frequent use data.

## Structure — Split Top and Bottom Compartment

- Separate radiator channel
- Motherboard compartment independent cooling holes

## Dimensions

233mm(W) x 262mm(D) x 298mm(H)

Removable unify top cover. Easier to install without the constraints of the case.

Multi-disc pull-out structure with PCB adapter plate. Hot-swappable for quick hard drive replacement.

## Server-grade Hot-Swappable Backplane

Adopts server-grade 10μm gold-plated SAS hot-swappable interface to ensure long service life.

- All solid-state long-life capacitors to ensure stable power supply and prolong the life of hard discs
- D-type power supply plug
- SATA power supply header (auxiliary)
- Front HDD indicator socket
- Dual D-type plugs with gold-plated solid pins ensure strong, long-lasting, antioxidant power supply
- FAN socket

*It is recommended that the D-type/SATA power supply ports on the hot-swap be connected to the corresponding power supply.

## Cooling Fan Position

Independent cooling channels for hard drive and motherboard compartments, and separate cooling channels without interference.

- Motherboard compartment: 80x25mm x2 (optional)
- HDD compartment: 100x25mm x2 (standard)
- HDD compartment fan also supports 90x25mm specification

## Installation Illustrations

- Hard Drives: 8x 3.5" HDD hot-swappable drive bays, plus 1x 2.5" SSD drive bay

- Power supply: SFX power supply ≤105mm
